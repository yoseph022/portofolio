Willify

Willify is a web-based music application that empowers users to create, organize, and enjoy personalized music playlists. With its intuitive and user-friendly interface, Willify offers an interactive listening experience, allowing users to tailor their playlists to their unique tastes. The app also features smart recommendations, helping users discover new music that resonates with their preferences. Whether you're a casual listener or a music enthusiast, Willify transforms the way you experience music, making it more enjoyable and personalized than ever before.

Alcia

Alcia is an intelligent application designed to assist prospective students in discovering the best scholarship opportunities available at Indonesian universities. Not only does Alcia provide comprehensive information about various scholarships, but it also features a unique interactive questionnaire that engages users in a series of thought-provoking questions. This innovative approach aims to deliver personalized recommendations for academic programs that align with their interests, talents, and potential. With Alcia, finding the right path to higher education has never been easier or more tailored to individual aspirations.

VFR

Virtual Fitting Room (VFR) is an innovative application that allows users to try on clothes virtually before making a purchase. Utilizing cutting-edge technology, VFR enables users to visualize how their selected garments will look and fit on their bodies—without the hassle of physically trying them on. This revolutionary shopping experience makes it more practical, convenient, and enjoyable for users to discover outfits that perfectly match their size and style. With VFR, the future of fashion shopping is at your fingertips, transforming the way you shop for clothes!

Puff And Poof

Puff And Poof is a dedicated app designed for doll enthusiasts. This user-friendly platform makes it easy and secure for users to buy and sell a wide variety of dolls. With Puff And Poof, discovering your dream doll collection or finding a new home for your beloved dolls becomes a practical and enjoyable experience. Explore an extensive range of dolls from all corners of the world and connect with fellow collectors along the way. Whether you're looking to expand your collection or share your passion, Puff And Poof brings the doll community together in a fun and interactive way!

Fio

Figure It Out (FIO) is a specialized app for buying and selling collectibles, tailored for figure collectors and enthusiasts. With FIO, users can effortlessly discover a wide array of exclusive figures, ranging from beloved characters to rare collectibles. The app also provides a secure and swift platform for users to sell their own figures, making it the perfect place for collectors to build and share their collections. Whether you're on the hunt for that elusive piece or looking to showcase your prized items, FIO connects you with a vibrant community of fellow collectors, enhancing your collecting journey!
